Sophie Murguia is an assistant editor at Outside Magazine, where she works on the research and culture teams. Previously, she was an associate editor on the investigations desk at Pacific Standard, and before that she wrote and fact-checked for Mother Jones. She writes about immigration, politics, books, gender, and the outdoors.

For freelance assignments, you can reach her via email at sophiemurguia@gmail.com. To pitch her a culture story at Outside, email culture@outsideim.com. She's also on Twitter at @sophiemurguia.
